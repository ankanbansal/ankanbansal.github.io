My research interest lies in scene understanding problems in computer vision. During my PhD, I worked on problems related to visual question answering, zero-shot learning, human-object interactions, and face analysis. My PhD advisor was Prof. Rama Chellappa.

I completed my bachelor's and master's at Indian Institute of Technology, Kanpur. I did my master's thesis on estimating number of people in images of high density crowds under Prof. K. S. Venkatesh and Prof. Laxmidhar Behera.
